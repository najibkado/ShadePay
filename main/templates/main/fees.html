Fees and Pricing

New Transaction Fees

1.5% + NGN 20 for Local Transaction

For local transactions, ShadePay charges 1.5% plus NGN 20 fixed price. Fees are capped at NGN 3,500

4.5% + NGN 50 for International Transaction

For international transactions, ShadePay charges 4.5% plus NGN 50 fixed price. Fees are capped at NGN 3,500

NGN 500 for Linking Account

ShadePay will charge you NGN 500 when linking your account. Funds will be added to your ShadePay wallet

NGN 20 for BVN Verification

ShadePay will charge you NGN 20 for BVN verification

1.5% for Internal Transaction

ShadePay will charge you 1.5% when sending funds within ShadePay. Fees are capped at NGN 2,000

NGN 20 for Withdraw

ShadePay will charge you NGN 20 for Withdrawing funds between 1,000 and below 5,000

NGN 50 for Withdraw

ShadePay will charge you NGN 50 for Withdrawing funds between 5,001 and below 50,000

NGN 100 for Withdraw

ShadePay will charge you NGN 100 for Withdrawing funds between 50,001 and above

NGN 100 for Arguments

ShadePay will charge you NGN 100 service fee for creating an argument
