Shadepay Saving Wallet

User Story

User has a wedding or other event that is coming up soon? User has to pay school fees? However you user is finding it difficult to save money up for the event?

Accept Payments

Shadepay gives you the power to accept payments in different forms, either from your friends and family by sharing your wallet address, or you can share your shadepay link.

Solution Plans

Shadepay provides you with a solution. Create a saving wallet, Set a date for when you want to withdraw your funds, and you can only withdraw your funds when it is time.

Withdraw

Shadepay lets you withdraw your funds when the time you set for yourself is due. However in case of an emergency, be sure to reachout to shadepay support to release your funds for you to withdraw.
